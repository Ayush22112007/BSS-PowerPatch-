Why Transformer Oil Leaks Are Critical


Unplanned Downtime

Transformer shutdowns can occur within minutes, causing production loss and operational disruption.


Fire & Safety Risk

Leakage near energized components increases risk of arc flash and fire hazards.

Environmental Liability

Oil spills lead to contamination, regulatory penalties, and costly cleanup operations.

Equipment Degradation

Continuous leakage reduces insulation efficiency and shortens equipment lifespan.
